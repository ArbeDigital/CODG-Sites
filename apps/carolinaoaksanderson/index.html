About Us

Meet Dr. Vaughn Layne

Dr. Vaughn Layne D.D.S.

Dr. Layne grew up in dentistry as both her mother and father are dentists. She has had the opportunity to observe the wonderful care that her parents have provided over the years. She graduated from Furman University with a BS degree in Health and Exercise Science and then pursued her Doctorate of Dental Surgery from West Virginia University School of Dentistry. Dr. Layne met her husband, Cameron, at Furman University and they now reside in the upstate with their daughter and two Golden Doodles.

Dr. Layne enjoys establishing a unique patient relationship built on a foundation of mutual trust and respect. She believes that by listening to patient questions and concerns, she will best continue her mission of assisting and encouraging patients to achieve outstanding dental health.

Through Dr. Layne’s commitment and dedication to quality dental care, she pledges to provide the very best that dentistry has to offer. She believes that her patients expect and deserve excellence of care in any dental situation. For this reason, she would like to take this opportunity to ask for your trust and confidence in referring your friends, coworkers, and family members to Carolina Oaks Dental Care of Anderson.

Meet Dr. Thomas Layne III

Dr. Thomas Layne III D.D.S.

For Dr. Thomas Layne, patients are like family. Throughout his 34 years of practicing dentistry, he has remained passionate about improving smiles, restoring function, and helping patients build confidence through exceptional dental care. He has dedicated his entire career to providing the highest level of quality, compassion, and personalized attention to every patient he serves.

Dr. Layne considers himself fortunate to work alongside a team whose caring personalities and genuine commitment help patients feel comfortable and at ease. He enjoys making a positive impact in each patient’s life by providing education and preventive guidance that supports both oral health and overall wellness.

Dr. Layne earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from West Virginia University in 1979 and is a member of numerous local and national dental organizations. Committed to lifelong learning, he continually pursues advanced education on the latest dental technologies and treatment methods to ensure patients receive the most current and effective care available.

Meet Dr. Tanner Scaggs

Dr. Tanner Scaggs, DMD

Dr. Tanner Scaggs is originally from North Augusta, SC and now lives in Greenville with his wife, Anna, and their golden retriever, Cooper. He earned a Bachelor of Science in Biological Sciences from Clemson University and completed his dental training at the Dental College of Georgia at Augusta University.

After spending his college years here, Dr. Scaggs is excited to serve the Upstate SC community. He values building trust through empathetic care and takes time to understand each patient’s needs.

Outside the office, he enjoys walks, hikes, and adventures with his wife and dog, plus a good cup of coffee and traveling the globe!
