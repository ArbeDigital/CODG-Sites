About Us

Meet the experienced and friendly doctors

Dr. Susan H. Maxwell, DMD

Born and raised in Greenville, Dr. Susan Maxwell earned her Doctorate of Dental Medicine from the Medical University of South Carolina in Charleston. Following graduation, she completed a general practice residency in Charlotte at the Carolinas Medical Center. Her residency was heavily focused on oral medicine and surgery. Dr. Maxwell has been with Carolina Oaks since 2012 and is known for providing compassionate, knowledgeable care.

Meet Dr. Maxwell

Dr. Harrison Crouch, DMD

Dr. Harrison Crouch, a Clemson and MUSC graduate from Florence, SC, has dreamed of being a dentist since middle school. Now serving the Greenville community, he’s dedicated to improving oral health and delivering exceptional care.
