About Us

Meet the experienced and friendly doctors

Dr. Molly J. Netzler, DMD

As the daughter of a general dentist, Dr. Molly Netzler developed a love of dentistry at an early age. The Summerville native attended dental school at the Medical University of South Carolina. While there, she was one of 15 students chosen nationwide to train and do dental research at the National Institute of Dental and Craniofacial Research in Bethesda, MD. Dr. Netzler has been practicing at our Clemson location since 2014 and is thrilled to have transitioned to our Greenville office.

Meet Dr. Netzler

Dr. Tait G. Carpenter, DMD

Dr. Tait Carpenter grew up in Summerville, SC and went on to earn her Doctor of Dental Medicine Degree from the Medical University of South Carolina. Most recently, her continuing education has focused on teeth straightening, cosmetic dentistry and TMD treatment. Dr. Carpenter began practicing at the Carolina Oaks’ Clemson location in 2016. She is excited to now be offering general dentistry in Greenville and loves helping patients of all ages achieve healthy smiles.

Meet Dr. Carpenter
